Origin

The Story
So Far

Growing up in Minnesota, severe weather was part of life. Tornado watches, storm sirens, the electric feeling of a sky about to do something dramatic — I was always drawn to it. What started as a fascination became a passion, and eventually a calling.

I began storm chasing seriously in 2024, joining Tornado Tacklers — a network of dedicated chasers committed to documenting severe weather and keeping communities informed. I now serve as Team Captain, helping coordinate chases and manage our growing media presence.

Along the way I discovered something unexpected: building the technology behind the content was just as exciting as the chase itself. API integrations, live streaming platforms, AI-powered alert systems — I taught myself by doing, using AI as a collaborator to turn visual ideas into working code.

Before storm chasing, I spent 5+ years in Human Resources — coordinating HR operations, managing HRIS systems, handling onboarding and offboarding, and ensuring compliance across organizations. That background gave me a deep appreciation for systems, process, and people — skills that translate directly into running a 30-chaser organization and building user-focused platforms.

On June 20, 2025, I documented multiple tornadoes across North Dakota — from Spiritwood during the day to Fort Ransom after dark. That same day I was featured on NewsNation to discuss the outbreak. It was a defining moment that brought everything together: the chasing, the content, the reach.

Now I'm building Storm Current — a live streaming platform for storm chasers launching in spring 2026 — while continuing to chase, create, and grow the Tornado Tacklers brand to new heights.
