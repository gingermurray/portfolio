THE DAY
A historic outbreak across North Dakota — multiple tornado touchdowns documented from afternoon into the night. One of the most active chase days on record for the region.
LOCATIONS
Daytime tornadoes near Spiritwood, ND. After dark, additional tornadoes near Fort Ransom, ND. The stunning supercell photographed near Coburn, ND at sunset.
MEDIA
Coverage from this chase day was featured on NewsNation during their live broadcast of the outbreak, reaching at least 3 fatalities across the state.
